The WWE Universal Championship is a world heavyweight championship created and promoted by the American professional wrestling promotion WWE, currently defended on their SmackDown brand division. It is one of WWE's top titles, alongside the WWE Championship on Raw. The current champion is Roman Reigns, who is in his second reign.
